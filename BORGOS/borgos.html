ABOUT SONAPUR COLLEGE


Sonapur College, established in the year 1991, has successfully completed 25 years of its glorious journey and celebrating Silver Jubilee in manner befitting to the mission, vision and objectives.

As a centre of academic excellence in the entire North East, the aim of the college is to nurture simultaneous academic and co-curricular endeavor. Since its inception, Sonapur College, from its nascent stage to a centre of academic excellence, has proved to be a meeting point for students from diverse ethnic and linguistic backgrounds of the region and beyond.

The College apart from education also intends to foster a spirit of academic excellence with the belief that value-based learning is of great relevance in a dynamic work environment. Students of the college enjoy an easy access to faculty guidance, full staff support, and constant monitoring and supervision at every level of their achieving excellence in their academic pursuits. The college provides students with a range of avenues and opportunities for self-expression, initiative and experimentation in both the intellectual and co-curricular fields. Read More
